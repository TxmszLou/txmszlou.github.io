What’s Augmented Reality?

Brief introduction:

This small allow you to track views from smartphone’s camera.

If the desired image(token) is found through the camera, it will display a 3d model on to the screen.

The screen view of the 3d model will change according to the relative position of the token image and the camera.

Thanks anynomous for the 3d model of Christina.

How?

After installing the app, open it and begin tracking by pressing “START!!” button.

Point the camera to the token image. (Here I used nVidia’s logo (￣▽￣”)).

Tab the “christina” button to display the 3d model.
