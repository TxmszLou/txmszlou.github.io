Part One The Dimensions of Reading

1. The Activity and Art of Reading

We don’t have to know every thing about some thing in order to understand it.

Active reading:

There is no conplete passive reading, but reading can be more or less active. The more active you read, the better.

What does active reading entail?

The Goals of Reading:

Reading for information and reading for understanding.

You are reading for information when you understand every thing perfectly while you are reading.

When you don’t complete understand what you are reading (communications between inequalities), you’re on the state of understanding less. Either you try to ask for External help or you try to use your Inner abilities to gain more understanding, you are on the state of understanding more. The skills used to transform ourselves from understanding less to understanding more is what we called the art of reading.

The art of reading: The process whereby a mind, with nothing to operate on but the symbols of the readable matter, and with no help from outside, elevates itself by the power of its own operations

One more thing…

Reading for entertainment: It is the least demanding kind of reading which requires least effort. You can say that is a waste of time.
